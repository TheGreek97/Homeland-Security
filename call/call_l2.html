Scenery

In this type of scams, the caller typically says they are from a well-known company like Microsoft or Apple and have detected an error on a person's computer. They will then talk to the victim through a series of steps to "fix" the problem. In reality, a person is unwittingly downloading software that will hijack their system or give the caller remote access. Scammers use it to gather sensitive data or install ransomware, which requires a payment to unlock a computer's files. After you listen to the recording, you have to choose between several possible "scam" words and put them inside the Scam basket words.
